4. Ce qui est en place — Fonctionnel

P1 Editorial (50 agents)
Generation SEO/AEO/GEO avec contexte SERP imperatif. 8 types de structure. LSI, FAQ auto, recherche vocale. Anti-hallucination. Qualite linguistique FR. SEO local. Schema auto. ~$0.60/article via Sonnet.
P2 Audit Contenu (12 agents)
7 dimensions SEO/AEO/GEO/EEAT/UX. Radar chart. Recommandations type-aware.
P3 Audit Technique (23 agents)
12 dimensions. 10 outils OSS. Crawl, schemas, performance, securite, hreflang. $0.00/10 pages.
P4 SERP (16 agents)
Positions GSC. Quick wins. Core Updates detection. Concurrents 7 types. AEO PAA tracking. Backlinks live. Benchmark concurrentiel.
P5 Strategie (24 agents)
Roadmap editoriale. Forecast 12 mois. Kill list. Confidence scoring + Decision trace. CEO summary. P5->P1 auto. CTR reformulator. Content gaps.
P6 Backlinks (18 agents)
CRM netlinking 8 statuts. AI link intelligence (llms.txt, 11 AI crawlers). Anchor strategy. Portfolio optimizer. Prospect discovery.
P7 Maintenance (15 agents)
Execution auto (sitemap+robots+llms+schemas+emails). CMS intervention 13 actions. Rapports auto. Pipeline editorial avec bloqueurs. Rollback.
P8 Learning (9 agents)
Accumulation silencieuse. Confidence calibration. Pattern detection. Delay estimation. Model distribution. 5 sources de donnees actives.
SaaS & Outils
12 outils SEO gratuits. WordPress plugin. Blog link. Disclaimers. Headless mode. DB backup. Docker+deploy. SECURITY.md.
UX/UI
Sidebar projet-centree (Linear/Stripe-inspired). Logo. Green dot projet actif. Welcome screen. Diagnostic 1-clic. 12 pages adaptatives.
